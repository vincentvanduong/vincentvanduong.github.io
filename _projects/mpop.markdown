#### Calculus and Mathematics
* Michael Spivak - Calculus
* Paul Zeitz - The Art and Craft of Problem Solving

#### Mechanics
* Daniel Kleppner, Robert J Kolenkow - An Introduction to Mechanics
* David J. Morin - Introduction to Classical Mechanics

#### Electricity and Magnetism
* Edward M. Purcell, David J. Morin - Electricity and Magnetism
* David J. Griffiths - Introduction to Electrodynamics

#### Thermodynamics and Statistical Mechanics
* Daniel V. Schroeder - An Introduction to Thermal Physics

#### Problem Books
* I.E. Irodov - Problems in General Physics
* S.S. Krotov - Aptitude Test Problems in Physics
